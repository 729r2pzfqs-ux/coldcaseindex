Holly Maddux

Status Conviction
Type Homicide
Date September 1977
Location Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
Victim Age 30
Gender Female

Holly Maddux disappeared in September 1977. Her mummified body was found in a trunk in her boyfriend Ira Einhorn's Philadelphia apartment in 1979. Einhorn, a prominent counterculture figure, fled to Europe before trial. He was convicted in absentia in 1993 and extradited from France in 2001, serving life in prison until his death in 2020.
